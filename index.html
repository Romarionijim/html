The Boston Celtics announced Tuesday that guard Kemba Walker received a stem-cell injection in his left knee in October and was placed on a 12-week strengthening program. The Celtics added that Walker will return to on-court activities in early December, and they will provide an update regarding his game availability in January's first week. Since the 2020-21 season is set to begin Dec. 22, Walker will miss the start of the campaign.
